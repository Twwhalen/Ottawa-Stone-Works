Our Story

A Quarter Century of Stone Craftsmanship

Ottawa Stone Works was founded on a simple belief: every property deserves a beautiful, enduring outdoor space. For over 25 years, our family-owned business has served the Orleans and Rockland communities with pride, skill, and an unwavering commitment to quality.

From elegant front entranceways and multi-level patios to intricate walkways and raised garden beds, every project is approached with the same care and attention to detail that has earned us a reputation as one of Ottawa's most trusted masonry companies.

We work exclusively with premium interlock and natural stone products, ensuring that each project not only looks stunning on day one — but continues to impress for decades to come.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before starting your project.

Most residential patio projects take between 3 to 7 days depending on the size and complexity. A straightforward backyard patio can be completed in as little as 2–3 days, while larger projects with custom steps, raised walls, and multiple levels may take up to a week or more. We'll give you a clear timeline during your free estimate.
We work from spring through fall — typically May through October. Spring and early summer are ideal since the ground is thawed and stable. That said, fall installations are perfectly fine and can sometimes be scheduled faster since demand slows down. We don't recommend winter installations due to frost and ground conditions.
Yes. We stand behind our work. We offer a warranty on labour and installation, and the interlock products we use come with manufacturer warranties. If you ever notice settling, shifting, or any issue related to installation, we'll come back and make it right. Just give us a call.
Every project is different, so we always recommend a free on-site estimate for an accurate quote. As a general guide, most residential patios in the Orleans and Rockland area range from $8,000 to $25,000+ depending on size, design complexity, materials chosen, and whether steps or walls are included. We'll walk you through all your options with no obligation.
In most cases, a standard interlock patio does not require a permit in Orleans or Rockland. However, retaining walls over a certain height, or work close to your property line, may require one. We're familiar with local requirements and can advise you during your consultation.
Interlock is very low maintenance. We recommend re-sanding the joints every few years as polymeric sand can break down over time. Sealing your interlock every 2–3 years helps preserve the colour and protects against staining. Avoid using salt in winter — use sand or a calcium-based product instead. We're happy to share full care instructions after your project is complete.
Our primary service area is Orleans and Rockland, where we've been working for 25 years. We also take on select projects in neighbouring communities like Cumberland, Navan, Vars, Embrun, and Russell. If you're unsure whether we cover your area, just give us a call — we're happy to discuss it.
We come to your property, assess the space, discuss your ideas and budget, and give you a detailed written quote — all at no cost and no obligation. We'll help you choose the right materials, layout, and features to make the most of your outdoor space. The estimate visit typically takes 30–45 minutes.
